Our Mission

The mission of the Thatcher Arizona Chamber of Commerce is to advance the quality of life for everyone in The Gila Valley through collaboration, leadership, and tourism by recruiting and supporting an exemplary, professional, and ethical team.

Our Vision

The Thatcher Arizona Chamber of Commerce cultivates the dreams of Gila Valley residents and guides those dreams to become successful realities.
